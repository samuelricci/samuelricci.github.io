Blackjack: '21'

Simple to learn but rewarding to master, Blackjack is your best bet at winning when gambling in a casino. Originating in France around the 1760's it is one of the most popular games for professional and casual gamblers alike. Keep reading to learn the basics and ways to become a better player improving your odds and postential payout.

Basics:

The goal of the game Blackjack is to get as close to 21 without going over while having a higher number than the house.
Each card is worth it's face value except for J's, Q's, K's and A's. An easy reference can be found below:

A round of betting preceds the initial deal afterwhich each player is dealt one card face up in a clockwise rotation, including the dealer. This can be followed by another round of betting but most often by another card dealt face up to each player with one face down to the dealer.
